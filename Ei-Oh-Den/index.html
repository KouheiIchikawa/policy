Ei-Oh-Den ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

The App does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, or identifiers.

The App does not use third-party advertising SDKs and does not use analytics SDKs.

The App is a paid app (one-time purchase) distributed via Google Play. Payment processing is handled by Google Play. The App itself does not collect or store your payment information.

Permissions

The App does not require special permissions to access sensitive device data (e.g., camera, microphone, location, contacts).

Data Sharing

Because the App does not collect data, it does not share data with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
